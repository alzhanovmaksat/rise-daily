Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save an affirmation to my favorites?

Simply tap the heart icon on any affirmation card. You can view all your saved favorites in the Favorites tab at the bottom of the app.

How does the daily streak work?

Your streak increases each day you open the app. If you miss a day, the streak resets to 1, but your longest streak record is always saved. It's a great way to build a consistent daily habit!

Can I filter affirmations by category?

Yes! On the home screen, you'll see category chips (Faith, Confidence, Gratitude, Peace, Strength, Hope). Tap any category to filter affirmations, or tap "All" to see everything.

How do I turn off notifications?

Go to the Settings tab and toggle off "Daily Notifications". You can also manage notification permissions in your device's Settings app.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data (favorites, streaks, preferences) is stored only on your device. We don't collect any personal information or send data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I share an affirmation?

Tap the "Share" button below any affirmation to share it via text message, email, social media, or any other app on your device.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Restart your device, 3) Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store. If issues persist, please contact us!
